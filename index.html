In this tournament we've aimed to simplify things since we're brand new to running things. To that end we've written up All About Duels of the Spellslingers with yellow highlights on anything notably new.

Tournament 5 Rules

This time the rules are simple. You may use any planeswalker and cards you wish and you may switch planeswalker/cards between each individual game. Each match will be for best 2 out of 3 games. You wonâ€™t ever know what deck your opponent will choose!

Tournament 5 Structure

The tournament will take place in two stages; a Group Stage and a Final Stage. We will use Challonge to randomly assign and notify people of their groups/opponents, and publicize the winner/loser here on this website.

Group Stage

Players will be randomly assigned to small groups where each group will play a single-elimination format. We will likely have a total of four groups each sending two participants to the Final Stage.

Final Stage

The Final Stage will be a single-elimination format consisting of eight players who have advanced from the Group Stage.

Prize

There will be a secret prize for the winner of Tournament 5!

Happy slinging everyone!

About

Duels of the Spellslingers is a group of enthusiasts of the game Magic Spellslingers from Wizards of the Coast. We organize regular fun tournaments for which we record the games and publish commentated videos of the tournament matches. Anyone is welcome!
